Japanese Curry

Description

Cold? locked down? hungry? This japanese curry will help you find your inner quarantined peace. Its actually really easy to make (as long as you can cut a potato)

You can find the Japanese curry cubes at pretty much any grocery store these days. There is one i really prefer that has apple and honey (yum!) infused in it.

  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Potato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Broccoli
  • 1/2 packet of Japanese Curry Cubes
  • 4 cups of water
  1. Cut vegetables into bite sized pieces
  2. Sautee in dutch oven and fill with water over medium heat to a simmer for 40 minutes
  3. Turn off heat. Add Japanese 6 curry cubes and simmer for 15 minutes. You're done!
  4. Eat with sticky rice
